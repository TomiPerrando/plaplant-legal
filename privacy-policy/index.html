Privacy Policy - Plaplant

Last updated: February 11, 2026

App: Plaplant

Operator: Tomas Perrando (DataTomi)

Privacy contact: plaplantchat@gmail.com

1) What the app does

Plaplant helps users create and manage plant information and chat with an assistant for care guidance.

2) Data we process

  • Account data: email address, authentication/session data.
  • In-app content: plants, events, chat messages and responses.
  • Technical data: request metadata, timestamps, operational logs, IP in infrastructure logs.
  • Photos selected in app can be used for plant profile experience.

3) How we use data

  • Provide sign-in and app functionality.
  • Store plants and events.
  • Generate chat responses.
  • Operate, secure and improve service reliability.

4) Data sharing

We share data only with providers required to operate the service:

  • Supabase (authentication and data storage).
  • OpenAI (chat processing).
  • Cloud hosting/infrastructure providers for backend operations.

5) Ads and analytics

Plaplant currently does not use third-party advertising SDKs or marketing analytics SDKs.

6) Data retention

Data is retained while account/service is active and as needed for legal, security, and troubleshooting obligations.

7) Your rights

You can request access, correction or deletion of your data by writing to plaplantchat@gmail.com.

8) Account deletion

Users can delete their account directly in-app from Profile - Delete account.

See deletion details here: Account deletion page.

9) Children

Plaplant is not specifically directed to children.

10) Security

Data is transmitted using encrypted transport in production and handled with reasonable safeguards.

11) Policy changes

We may update this policy and revise the date shown above.
